## Why Move to Tampa?

Tampa is the heart of Tampa Bay — a booming metro with warm weather year-round, no state income tax, and a cost of living that still beats most major cities. Whether you're relocating for work, retirement, or a fresh start, Tampa delivers.

## Best Neighborhoods

- **South Tampa** — Walkable, upscale, close to Bayshore Boulevard
- **Seminole Heights** — Historic charm, craft breweries, eclectic vibes
- **Westchase** — Family-friendly master-planned community
- **New Tampa** — Suburban feel with easy interstate access
- **Water Street** — Downtown's newest urban district

## Cost of Living

Tampa's cost of living runs about 3–5% above the national average, but no state income tax offsets that quickly. Median home prices hover around $400K, with plenty of options from $250K starter homes to $1M+ waterfront estates.

## Getting Around

Tampa is a car-dependent city, but it's improving. The Selmon Expressway, I-275, and I-4 are the main arteries. The HART bus system covers basics, and the free TECO streetcar runs through downtown and Ybor City.

## Schools

Hillsborough County Public Schools is one of the largest districts in the country. Top-rated options include Plant High School, Robinson IB, and numerous charter and private schools.

## FAQs

### Is Tampa a good place to live?

Yes — Tampa consistently ranks among the best places to live in Florida. Strong job market, great food scene, pro sports teams, and easy beach access make it a top pick.

### How far is Tampa from the beach?

The closest Gulf beaches (Clearwater, St. Pete Beach) are about 30–45 minutes from downtown Tampa.
