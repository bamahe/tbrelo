## Tips for New Florida Residents and Their Vehicles

A few things to keep in mind after your move:

**Get your AC checked.** If you're coming from up north, your AC system may not have been worked hard in years. Florida summers will expose any weakness. Auto Rx can inspect and service your AC before the heat hits.

**Florida roads are tough on brakes.** Between the stop-and-go traffic, the rain, and the flat terrain that doesn't give you natural engine braking on hills, your brake pads will wear faster than you're used to.

**Update your registration and insurance within 30 days.** Florida requires it. While you're getting settled, having a mechanic who can handle your state inspection needs is one less thing to worry about.

**Ram, Ford, Chevy truck owners:** If you're driving a truck down from another state, get it checked after the move. Long-haul drives can surface issues that weren't apparent in city driving. Ryan specializes in truck work, especially Ram trucks with the 5.7L Hemi.
