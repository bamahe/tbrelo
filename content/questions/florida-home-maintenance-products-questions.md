Maintaining a home in Florida is a different game than anywhere else in the country. Between the humidity, the rain, the bugs, and the storms, your maintenance product list looks nothing like what you'd need up north. These 25 questions cover the specific products that keep a Florida home in good shape year-round.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What pressure washer do I need for my Florida driveway?

Florida driveways grow black algae and mildew within months thanks to the humidity and rain. A [3000 PSI Electric Pressure Washer](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=3000+PSI+electric+pressure+washer+home+driveway&tag=tbrelo-20) handles concrete driveways, pavers, and sidewalks without the hassle of gas maintenance. Electric models are strong enough for algae removal and easier to store. Use a [Surface Cleaner Attachment for Pressure Washer](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=surface+cleaner+attachment+pressure+washer+15+inch&tag=tbrelo-20) to clean driveways evenly without leaving stripe marks. Plan on pressure washing your driveway and walkways at least twice a year in Florida — once in spring and once in fall.

### What pool cleaner robot is best for Florida pools?

Florida pools collect leaves, pollen, and sand nonstop, and the warm water breeds algae fast without constant circulation. A [Robotic Pool Cleaner Automatic](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=robotic+pool+cleaner+automatic+inground+wall+climbing&tag=tbrelo-20) runs independently, climbs walls, and scrubs the waterline where algae builds up. Dolphin and Polaris are the brands Florida pool owners trust most. The robot handles the daily debris so your filter and pump system can focus on water chemistry. Run it 2-3 times a week in summer — Florida pools work harder than pools anywhere else.

### What AC filter should I use in Florida humidity?

Your AC is the workhorse of your Florida home, running up to 12 hours daily in summer. A [MERV 11 Pleated AC Filter](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=MERV+11+pleated+AC+filter+air+furnace&tag=tbrelo-20) traps mold spores, pollen, and dust mites without restricting airflow. Don't go above MERV 13 unless your HVAC system is rated for it — many Florida systems lose efficiency with overly restrictive filters. Change your filter every 30 days in summer and every 60 in winter. Set a reminder on your phone. A clogged filter in Florida heat can freeze your evaporator coil and leave you without AC on a 97-degree day.

### What's the best weed killer for Florida lawns?

Florida lawns grow differently than northern grass — St. Augustine, Bahia, and Zoysia are the big three. A [Southern Lawn Weed Killer Herbicide](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=southern+lawn+weed+killer+St+Augustine+safe+herbicide&tag=tbrelo-20) formulated for warm-season grasses is essential because northern weed killers can kill your lawn. [Spectracide Weed Stop for Southern Lawns](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Spectracide+Weed+Stop+southern+lawns&tag=tbrelo-20) specifically targets Florida weeds like dollarweed, clover, and crabgrass without damaging St. Augustine. Apply in early morning before temperatures climb — herbicides applied in Florida midday heat burn grass.

### Do I need a mold test kit for my Florida home?

If you smell anything musty, yes. Florida homes are mold factories when humidity isn't controlled. A [Home Mold Test Kit with Lab Analysis](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=home+mold+test+kit+lab+analysis+air+surface&tag=tbrelo-20) lets you test surfaces and air quality, then mail the sample to a lab for species identification. This matters because black mold (Stachybotrys) requires professional remediation, while common Florida mold like Cladosporium can be handled with consumer products. Test your AC closet, behind toilets, and any areas that smell musty first. The kit costs around $30 but can save you from unknowingly breathing dangerous mold.

### What paint resists Florida humidity and mold?

Standard interior paint in a Florida bathroom will grow mold within a year. Use [Mold and Mildew Resistant Interior Paint](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=mold+mildew+resistant+interior+paint+bathroom+kitchen&tag=tbrelo-20) with antimicrobial additives for bathrooms, laundry rooms, and kitchens. For exterior paint, [Exterior Paint with UV and Mildew Protection](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=exterior+acrylic+paint+UV+mildew+resistant+Florida&tag=tbrelo-20) with UV stabilizers holds up years longer than standard paint in Florida's blistering sun and daily rain cycles. Sherwin-Williams Duration and Behr Ultra are both proven performers on Florida exteriors. Always use a mildew-resistant primer first on bare surfaces.

### What gutter guards work for Florida rain?

Florida gets 50+ inches of rain annually, often in sheets. Gutter guards need to handle volume, not just leaves. [Micro Mesh Gutter Guards](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=micro+mesh+gutter+guards+stainless+steel&tag=tbrelo-20) with stainless steel mesh let water flow through while blocking leaves, pine needles, and the roof grit that Florida storms wash down. Skip foam or brush-style guards — they clog with Florida pollen and trap moisture that grows mold. Make sure your downspouts are directed at least 4 feet away from your foundation. Florida's sandy soil erodes fast around foundations when gutters overflow.

### What's the best ant killer for Florida fire ants?

Florida fire ants build mounds overnight after rain, and their stings send people to the ER. [Fire Ant Killer Mound Treatment](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=fire+ant+killer+mound+treatment+granules&tag=tbrelo-20) applied directly to mounds kills the colony within 24 hours. For yard-wide prevention, [Fire Ant Broadcast Bait Granules](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=fire+ant+broadcast+bait+granules+yard&tag=tbrelo-20) spread across the lawn prevent new mounds from forming. Apply bait in the morning or evening when ants are foraging, not during Florida's midday heat when they stay underground. Treat your entire yard twice a year — spring and late summer — to stay ahead of new colonies migrating from neighboring properties.

### Do I need a pool cover in Florida?

A pool cover in Florida isn't about keeping heat in like up north — it's about keeping debris out. A [Solar Pool Cover](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=solar+pool+cover+16+mil+blue&tag=tbrelo-20) reduces evaporation (you can lose an inch of water per week in Florida summer heat), cuts chemical usage by blocking UV from breaking down chlorine, and keeps leaves out. A 16-mil cover lasts longer than thinner options in Florida sun. You'll also notice fewer bugs in the skimmer basket. For safety reasons, a solar cover is not a safety cover — if you need pool safety fencing for children, that's a separate and legally required item.

### What lawn mower works best for Florida grass?

Florida St. Augustine grass needs to be cut at 3.5 to 4 inches — shorter than that invites weeds and burns in the sun. A [Self-Propelled Lawn Mower with Height Adjustment](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=self+propelled+lawn+mower+height+adjustment+21+inch&tag=tbrelo-20) with a range up to 4 inches handles Florida grass properly. Self-propelled is important because you're mowing in 90-degree heat and pushing a mower through thick St. Augustine is exhausting. [Battery Powered Lawn Mower 56V](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=battery+powered+lawn+mower+56V+self+propelled&tag=tbrelo-20) from EGO or Greenworks avoids the ethanol-gas problems that plague small engines in Florida's humid storage conditions.

### What's the best caulk for Florida bathroom mold?

Standard caulk in a Florida bathroom turns black with mold within 6 months. [Mold-Free Silicone Bathroom Caulk](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=mold+free+silicone+bathroom+caulk+GE+Momentive&tag=tbrelo-20) with antimicrobial protection lasts 5+ years in Florida humidity. GE Momentive silicone caulk is what most Florida contractors use because it stays flexible, sticks to wet-environment surfaces, and actively resists mold growth. Remove all old caulk completely before reapplying — new caulk over old moldy caulk just traps moisture behind it. Clean the surface with bleach, let it dry fully, then apply.

### Do I need a whole-house water filter in Florida?

Florida water is among the hardest and most mineral-heavy in the country. A [Whole House Water Filter System](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=whole+house+water+filter+system+sediment+chlorine&tag=tbrelo-20) installed at your main water line removes sediment, chlorine, and odor from every faucet, shower, and appliance. This protects your water heater, washing machine, and dishwasher from mineral buildup that shortens their lives. If you're on well water, add a [Well Water Iron and Sulfur Filter](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=well+water+iron+sulfur+filter+whole+house&tag=tbrelo-20) to eliminate that rotten-egg smell. A whole-house system pays for itself by extending appliance life in Florida's harsh water conditions.

### What roof sealant works for Florida storms?

Florida roofs take relentless UV, driving rain, and occasional hurricane-force winds. [Roof Sealant Elastomeric Coating](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=roof+sealant+elastomeric+coating+waterproof+UV&tag=tbrelo-20) creates a waterproof membrane that expands and contracts with Florida's temperature swings. For quick repairs before a storm, [Roof Leak Repair Sealant Tape](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=roof+leak+repair+sealant+tape+waterproof&tag=tbrelo-20) provides temporary sealing around flashing, vents, and nail pops. Do a roof walk-through every spring before hurricane season starts — catching loose flashing or cracked sealant in May is a lot cheaper than dealing with a leak during a July thunderstorm.

### What's the best way to clean Florida pool tile?

Florida pool tile builds up calcium scale from the hard water and a white line from fluctuating water levels. A [Pool Tile Cleaning Pumice Stone](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=pool+tile+cleaning+pumice+stone+calcium+remover&tag=tbrelo-20) removes calcium scale without scratching tile — it's the simplest and cheapest method. For heavy buildup, [Pool Tile Calcium Remover Spray](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=pool+tile+calcium+remover+spray+scale&tag=tbrelo-20) dissolves deposits chemically. Spray it on, let it sit, then wipe. Maintain your pool's calcium hardness level between 200-400 ppm to slow scale formation. Florida's tap water already comes in high on calcium, so testing your fill water matters.

### Do I need a sump pump in Florida?

Most Florida homes don't have basements, but low-lying properties and areas with high water tables can flood during heavy rain. If water pools near your foundation or under your home, a [Submersible Sump Pump](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=submersible+sump+pump+1+3+HP+automatic+float+switch&tag=tbrelo-20) with an automatic float switch activates when water rises. Homes with enclosed lanais below grade or below-slab plumbing are particularly at risk during Florida's summer storms. If you're in a flood zone, a [Battery Backup Sump Pump](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=battery+backup+sump+pump+system&tag=tbrelo-20) keeps pumping even when the power goes out during a storm — exactly when you need it most.

### What outdoor fan works for a Florida lanai?

Your lanai is unusable in July without air movement. A [Wet Rated Outdoor Ceiling Fan 52 Inch](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=wet+rated+outdoor+ceiling+fan+52+inch+lanai+patio&tag=tbrelo-20) is engineered for rain exposure and humidity — standard indoor fans will rust and seize within a season. Look for "wet rated" specifically, not just "damp rated," because Florida lanais get direct rain during storms. Matte finishes and stainless hardware resist Florida's salt-air corrosion better than polished chrome. A good outdoor fan extends your lanai season from about 4 months to nearly year-round.

### What's the best driveway sealer for Florida heat?

Florida sun bakes asphalt driveways until they crack and fade. A [Driveway Sealer Asphalt UV Resistant](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=driveway+sealer+asphalt+UV+resistant+5+gallon&tag=tbrelo-20) protects against UV damage, water penetration, and the oil stains that Florida rain washes onto your driveway from parked cars. Apply in the early morning before the asphalt gets too hot — sealant applied on a 140-degree driveway surface won't cure properly. For paver driveways, use a [Paver Sealer Wet Look](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=paver+sealer+wet+look+UV+protection&tag=tbrelo-20) that enhances color and prevents the weed growth that Florida's year-round growing season encourages between pavers.

### Do I need hurricane shutters or impact windows?

If you live in Florida, you need one or the other. [Hurricane Shutter Panels Steel](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=hurricane+shutter+panels+steel+corrugated&tag=tbrelo-20) are the budget option — they bolt over windows before a storm and store in the garage between seasons. [Hurricane Fabric Window Protection](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=hurricane+fabric+window+protection+screens&tag=tbrelo-20) is lighter and easier to install alone than steel panels. Impact windows are a permanent upgrade that also lowers your insurance premium, but they cost $15,000-40,000 for a whole house. At minimum, have shutters or plywood pre-cut for every window before June 1. Storm prep isn't something you want to figure out when a hurricane is three days away.

### What weed trimmer handles Florida overgrowth?

Florida vegetation grows aggressively year-round. A [Battery Powered String Trimmer 56V](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=battery+powered+string+trimmer+56V+brushless&tag=tbrelo-20) with brushless motor handles thick Florida weeds without the gas-engine maintenance headaches that Florida humidity causes. EGO's 56V line is popular with Florida homeowners because the batteries work across their whole tool lineup. Use .095 or thicker trimmer line — Florida weeds like torpedo grass and bahia seed heads chew through thin line instantly. A [Bump Feed Trimmer Head](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=bump+feed+trimmer+head+universal+string&tag=tbrelo-20) lets you advance line without stopping to manually rethread.

### What's the best rust remover for Florida irrigation stains?

Florida irrigation water leaves orange rust stains on driveways, sidewalks, house siding, and anything within spray range. [Iron Out Rust Stain Remover Spray](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Iron+Out+rust+stain+remover+spray+outdoor&tag=tbrelo-20) dissolves the iron oxide stains without scrubbing — spray it on, let it sit for 5 minutes, and rinse. For prevention, a [Sprinkler Head Rust Filter](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=sprinkler+irrigation+rust+filter+iron&tag=tbrelo-20) on your irrigation system reduces the iron content before it sprays onto your surfaces. Adjust your sprinkler heads so they're not hitting your driveway, walkway, or house — this is the number one cause of curb appeal complaints in Florida neighborhoods.

### Do I need a smart sprinkler controller in Florida?

Florida has strict watering restrictions — most counties limit you to 2 days per week during certain hours. A [Smart Sprinkler Controller WiFi](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=smart+sprinkler+controller+WiFi+Rachio+weather+based&tag=tbrelo-20) like Rachio automatically skips watering after rain, adjusts for soil moisture, and ensures you stay within your county's watering schedule. This matters because Florida water districts issue fines for violations. The water savings alone pay for the controller within a year. Smart controllers also prevent overwatering — in Florida's sandy soil, excess water just drains past the root zone and wastes money.

### What deck stain survives Florida weather?

Florida decks face UV, rain, humidity, and mold every day of the year. A [Semi-Transparent Deck Stain UV Protection](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=semi+transparent+deck+stain+UV+protection+waterproof&tag=tbrelo-20) with UV blockers and mold inhibitors outperforms solid stains that peel in Florida humidity. TWP (Total Wood Preservative) and Ready Seal are the brands Florida deck owners swear by because they penetrate the wood instead of sitting on top. Prep work matters more than the stain itself — pressure wash, let the wood dry for 48 hours, then apply. In Florida, plan on re-staining every 2-3 years compared to every 4-5 up north.

### What's the best tool for removing Florida mildew from screens?

Florida screen enclosures and lanai screens turn green with mildew every rainy season. A [Screen Cleaning Brush Soft Bristle](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=screen+cleaning+brush+soft+bristle+window+patio&tag=tbrelo-20) with soft bristles cleans without tearing the mesh — stiff brushes and pressure washers destroy screen material. Spray the screens first with a [Screen Cleaner Mold Mildew Remover](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=screen+cleaner+mold+mildew+remover+outdoor&tag=tbrelo-20) or a diluted bleach solution (1 part bleach to 10 parts water), let it sit for 10 minutes, then gently brush and rinse. Clean your screens at least twice a year — once after rainy season and once in spring. Green screens tank your curb appeal fast.

### Do I need a battery backup for my Florida garage door?

When Florida power goes out during a storm, you can't open an electric garage door to evacuate or access your hurricane supplies. A [Garage Door Opener Battery Backup](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=garage+door+opener+battery+backup+LiftMaster+Chamberlain&tag=tbrelo-20) lets you open and close the door multiple times during a power outage. LiftMaster and Chamberlain make plug-in backup batteries compatible with most existing openers. This is especially important if your emergency supplies, generator, or vehicle is in the garage. You can manually disengage the opener in an emergency, but battery backup is safer and easier when you're dealing with storm stress. Check {{amazon}} for compatibility with your specific opener model.

### What solar lights work for Florida yards?

Florida's abundant sunshine makes solar lights a no-brainer for landscape lighting. [Solar LED Pathway Lights Stainless Steel](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=solar+LED+pathway+lights+stainless+steel+bright&tag=tbrelo-20) with stainless steel housings resist Florida's salt air and humidity better than plastic models. For accent lighting, [Solar Spotlights Outdoor Bright](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=solar+spotlights+outdoor+bright+landscape+waterproof&tag=tbrelo-20) aimed at palm trees or architectural features look great without any wiring. Get lights rated at least 200 lumens — cheap 10-lumen solar lights produce barely visible light. The key in Florida is positioning them where they get full sun during the day. Oak tree shade and screened lanai overhangs are the two biggest reasons solar lights fail to charge here.
