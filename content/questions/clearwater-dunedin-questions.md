Clearwater, Dunedin, Safety Harbor, and Palm Harbor sit along Pinellas County's Gulf coast and offer some of the best beach access, downtown walkability, and quality of life in Tampa Bay. Whether you're drawn to Clearwater's famous white sand or Dunedin's craft brewery scene, here's what you need to know before making a move.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Clearwater Beach the same as Clearwater?

No. Clearwater Beach is the barrier island on the Gulf side connected by the Memorial Causeway. The city of Clearwater sits on the mainland and has its own downtown, neighborhoods, and suburban areas. Prices on the beach side run significantly higher than mainland Clearwater.

### What is the average home price in Clearwater right now?

Clearwater's median home price fluctuates but generally sits below the Pinellas County average for single-family homes, making it more accessible than many coastal cities. Condos on or near the beach push that number up. For the most current pricing, reach out and I can pull real-time MLS data for you.

### Is Dunedin a good place to live for young professionals?

Absolutely. Dunedin's downtown is walkable, packed with craft breweries, restaurants, and shops along Main Street. The Pinellas Trail runs right through town, and the vibe attracts a younger crowd alongside retirees. Housing costs are moderate compared to St. Pete or Tampa.

### What makes Safety Harbor different from other Pinellas cities?

Safety Harbor has a small-town feel that most of Pinellas lost decades ago. The downtown is charming, the Safety Harbor Resort and Spa anchors the waterfront, and the community is tight-knit. It's also home to excellent parks and the Philippe Park area along Old Tampa Bay.

### How far is Palm Harbor from Tampa?

Palm Harbor sits in northern Pinellas County, roughly 25-30 miles from downtown Tampa. During rush hour, expect 45-60 minutes on US-19 or the Veterans Expressway. Off-peak, you can make it in about 30 minutes.

### Are there good schools in the Clearwater area?

Pinellas County Schools serves the entire area and offers several highly rated options. Countryside High, Palm Harbor University High, and Dunedin Highland Middle are popular choices. The county also has strong magnet and choice programs that let families apply across zones.

### What is the craft brewery scene like in Dunedin?

Dunedin is considered one of Florida's original craft beer towns. Dunedin Brewery claims to be the oldest craft brewery in the state, and you've also got 7venth Sun, Woodwright Brewing, and others within walking distance downtown. It's a legit draw for beer enthusiasts.

### Can I find waterfront homes in Safety Harbor?

Yes, but inventory is limited. Safety Harbor sits on Old Tampa Bay, so waterfront properties here offer bay views and access rather than Gulf beach frontage. Homes on the water go quickly and command a premium. If waterfront in Safety Harbor is your goal, be ready to move fast.

### What is Honeymoon Island like?

Honeymoon Island State Park is one of the top beaches in Florida. It's a barrier island accessible by causeway from Dunedin with natural beaches, trails, and a ferry to Caladesi Island, which consistently ranks among America's best beaches. Annual passes are worth it if you live nearby.

### Is Palm Harbor considered expensive?

Palm Harbor is mid-to-upper range for Pinellas County. You'll find everything from modest ranch homes to gated communities like Lansbrook and East Lake Woodlands. It's pricier than Spring Hill or New Port Richey but more affordable than Belleair or Clearwater Beach.

### What is the flooding risk in Clearwater?

Clearwater Beach and low-lying coastal areas carry significant flood risk, and flood insurance is required for most mortgages in those zones. Mainland Clearwater has pockets of flood zones, but many neighborhoods sit at higher elevations. Always check FEMA flood maps before buying.

### Are there condos near Clearwater Beach under $300,000?

They exist but are increasingly rare. Most affordable options are older buildings that may have higher HOA fees or pending special assessments. The condo insurance crisis in Florida has also driven up monthly costs. I always recommend reviewing the association's financials before making an offer.

### What is downtown Dunedin like on weekends?

It gets busy, especially during the cooler months. Live music, outdoor dining, and foot traffic along Main Street make it one of the liveliest small downtowns in Tampa Bay. Parking can be tight, but the city has added lots and the Pinellas Trail brings in cyclists and walkers.

### Is there a farmers market in the area?

Yes, several. Dunedin's downtown market runs on Fridays and Saturdays and is one of the best in the region. Safety Harbor also has a popular market. These aren't just produce stands — you'll find local vendors, prepared food, and artisan goods.

### How is the commute from Palm Harbor to St. Petersburg?

It's a haul. Expect 40-55 minutes depending on traffic, mostly via US-19 South or I-275. If you work in downtown St. Pete and live in Palm Harbor, the commute will test your patience during rush hour. Many people in Palm Harbor work in the northern Pinellas or Pasco area instead.

### What are the best neighborhoods in Clearwater for families?

Countryside, Cove Cay, and the area around Clearwater High tend to be popular with families. These neighborhoods offer good school access, parks, and suburban infrastructure without being too far from the beach. East Clearwater near US-19 has more affordable options.

### Does Dunedin have a downtown parking problem?

During peak season and weekends, yes. The city has worked on adding parking, but if you're going to a popular restaurant on a Saturday night, plan to walk a few blocks. During the week it's usually fine. Living downtown means you can skip the car entirely for most errands.

### What types of homes are common in Safety Harbor?

You'll see a mix of older Florida bungalows, mid-century ranches, and newer construction. The historic core near downtown has some of the most charming homes. Larger, newer builds are found on the outskirts. Safety Harbor doesn't have the cookie-cutter subdivision feel of some suburbs.

### Is there public transportation in Clearwater?

PSTA (Pinellas Suncoast Transit Authority) runs bus routes throughout Clearwater and connects to the rest of Pinellas County. The Jolley Trolley runs along the beach and into downtown. It's functional but most residents still rely on cars as their primary transportation.

### What is the job market like in Clearwater?

Clearwater is home to several large employers including BayCare Health System, Tech Data (now TD Synnex), and numerous hospitality businesses tied to tourism. The downtown area has been revitalizing with new office and mixed-use developments. Many residents commute to Tampa or St. Pete.

### Can I find a home with a pool in Palm Harbor under $400,000?

It's tight but possible, especially in older subdivisions. Pool homes in Palm Harbor's more established neighborhoods occasionally come on the market in that range. New construction with a pool will almost certainly exceed $400K. Let me run a search — inventory changes weekly.

### What is the Pinellas Trail and why does it matter for homebuyers?

The Pinellas Trail is a 75-mile paved recreational trail running through the county. Homes near trail access points in Dunedin, Palm Harbor, and Clearwater often see a bump in desirability and value. It's a major quality-of-life feature for cyclists, runners, and walkers.

### How bad are hurricanes in Clearwater?

Clearwater is in a hurricane-vulnerable zone, especially the beach and barrier islands. Evacuation zones are well-marked and the area has dealt with near-misses and direct impacts. Wind and storm surge are the biggest threats. Insurance costs reflect this reality, so factor that into your budget.

### Is Dunedin good for retirees?

Very much so. The walkable downtown, proximity to beaches and healthcare, moderate cost of living compared to other coastal towns, and active community make it a top pick for retirees. You'll find 55+ communities nearby too, though Dunedin proper attracts retirees who want to stay active and social.

### What is the rental market like in Clearwater?

Strong demand, especially near the beach where short-term vacation rentals compete with long-term tenants. Mainland Clearwater has more traditional rental options. If you're buying as an investment, pay close attention to local short-term rental regulations, which have been tightening.

### Are there any new construction communities in Palm Harbor?

Palm Harbor is largely built out, so new construction is limited compared to Pasco or eastern Hillsborough. You'll occasionally see infill projects or small developments, but if new construction is a priority, neighboring areas like East Lake or Oldsmar may offer more options.

### What is the food scene like in Dunedin and Clearwater?

Excellent for a metro area this size. Dunedin punches above its weight with spots like The Restorative, Frenchy's Outpost, and Casa Tina. Clearwater Beach has tourist-driven dining, but mainland Clearwater has solid local spots. Safety Harbor's downtown restaurant scene has grown significantly too.

### How does Palm Harbor compare to East Lake?

They overlap geographically, and many people use the names interchangeably. East Lake is technically an unincorporated area east of Palm Harbor with newer construction and top-rated schools like East Lake High. Palm Harbor proper has more established neighborhoods and a stronger sense of community identity.

### What should I know about HOA fees in Clearwater condos?

They can be steep, especially in older beach buildings dealing with structural inspections, insurance increases, and reserve requirements from Florida's post-Surfside condo safety laws. I've seen HOA fees jump $200-500/month in some buildings. Always request the full financials and reserve study before making an offer.
