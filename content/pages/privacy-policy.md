# Privacy Policy

**Site:** tbrelo.com, operated by Barrett Henry
**Effective date:** March 12, 2026

This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your options for controlling it. We wrote it in plain English on purpose.

## Information We Collect

We don't collect personal information unless you voluntarily provide it — for example, by filling out a contact form or sending us an email. If you do, we only use that information to respond to your inquiry.

We do collect anonymous analytics data automatically (described below).

## Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. This collects anonymous data such as:

- Pages visited and time spent on each page
- Device type and browser
- General geographic location (city-level, not your address)
- How you arrived at the site (search engine, social media, direct)

Google Analytics uses cookies to collect this data. No personally identifiable information is gathered through Analytics. You can read [Google's privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for more details on how they handle data, or install the [Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout) to stop tracking.

## Google AdSense

We may display ads through Google AdSense. Google uses cookies — including the DoubleClick cookie — to serve ads based on your visits to this site and other sites across the web.

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time:

- Visit [Google's Ad Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/)
- Or visit [aboutads.info](https://www.aboutads.info/choices/) to opt out of third-party ad cookies

## Affiliate Links

This site contains affiliate links (see our [Affiliate Disclosure](/affiliate-disclosure/) for details). When you click an affiliate link and visit a third-party site, that site may collect data according to their own privacy policy. We don't control how third-party sites handle your information.

## Cookies

We use cookies for analytics and advertising as described above. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help websites function and collect usage data.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Keep in mind that disabling cookies may affect how some websites function.

## Third-Party Links

Our site contains links to external websites for your reference and convenience. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. We encourage you to read the privacy policy of any site you visit.

## Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information to us, contact us at [barrett@nowtb.com](mailto:barrett@nowtb.com) and we will delete it promptly.

## Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any information collected through this site. That said, no data transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Your Rights — California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) to:

- Know what personal information we collect and how it is used
- Request deletion of your personal information
- Opt out of the sale of your personal information

We do not sell personal information. The anonymous analytics data collected through Google Analytics is not considered a sale of personal information under the CCPA. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [barrett@nowtb.com](mailto:barrett@nowtb.com).

## Your Rights — EU Visitors (GDPR)

If you are visiting from the European Union, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including:

- **Access:** You can request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
- **Rectification:** You can ask us to correct inaccurate data.
- **Erasure:** You can ask us to delete your personal data.
- **Restriction:** You can ask us to limit how we use your data.
- **Portability:** You can request your data in a portable format.
- **Objection:** You can object to data processing based on legitimate interests.

Our lawful basis for processing data is legitimate interest (understanding site traffic to improve content) and consent (when you voluntarily submit information through a form). To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [barrett@nowtb.com](mailto:barrett@nowtb.com).

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

## Contact
