C — Featured Image Cards

Each guide has a header image. Most visual and engaging. But requires 60+ images and significantly increases page load time. Could use AI-generated or stock images.

My recommendation: Option A (Clean & Minimal). With 60+ guides on one page, minimal cards keep it scannable and fast. Matches your Kadence card style. No image sourcing needed. You can always upgrade to B later.
